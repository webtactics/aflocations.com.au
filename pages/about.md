## Aussie Extensions deliver excellence in Home Extensions & Renovations. ##

### Excelent service, quality workmanship and value for money are the cornerstones of our business. ###


Aussie Extensions has been born from the idea of multi award winning builder Akira Homes, building brand new homes and extensions for the past 34 years, and have decided to concentrate wholly on new builds leaving the renovation and extension side with its own separate entity called Aussie Extensions. 

Anthony Kirillov of Aussie Extensions has won 5 Master Builder Awards including a National Award for the best extension under $100,000 in 2003.

Anthony Kirillov will continue building exceptional quality extensions/renovations under the name of Aussie Extensions which will be wholly dedicated to delivering the best quality renovations/extensions with value for money.

Anthony will design your renovation/extension for you, meeting all your requirements, taking the project from design through to the approval stage and finish.

You can be assured that your Aussie Extension will be second to none.  

Our promise is that we will deliver the best value for money in quality workmanship and design.
