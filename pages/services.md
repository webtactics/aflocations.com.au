<strong>Aussie Extensions can provide a range of servivces, including:</strong>


## Consultation: ## 

Aussie Extensions will guide you through the complicated process of planning and executing your new project. 

We will be there from beginning to end to ensue that your dream and vision are realised.


## Design: ## 

Aussie Extensions will help you create in depth designs, drawings and plans to ensure that you know how your vision will be realised, what trades and services you will need and how much to budget for your project.

## Construction: ##

Aussie Extensions will ensure that your build proceeds with quality workmanship, attention to detail and the utmost of safety.  All that is left for you to do is choose the design and direction of your project.

## Management: ##

Aussie Extensions will allocate resources, book tradespeople, manager relationship and handle invoicing. 

You can rest assured that everything happens within the desired time frame and budget.
