The existing house had a total Balinese makeover. A new pool complete with island with a thatched roof and a large rock waterfall. 

The kids didn’t miss out with a Balinese cubby house. 

The rear outdoor area was reshaped and tiled, with a timber laser light roof covering the patio. 

Tropical palms & plants were planted in the new pool area to give it a real Balinese feel. 

Next door loved it so much that we built them an outdoor thatched Balinese hut complete with bathroom & enclosed living area which fully opens up with bifold doors to 2 sides.
