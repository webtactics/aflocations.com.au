This modern new bathroom features floor to ceiling coloured glass panels in the shower and behind the vanity. 
The shower also has a glass alcove which houses the shampoo bottles and the concealed cover to the toilet cistern. 
Even the toilet roll holder is built into the wall and covered by a glass door. The toilet which has an in wall cistern and the vanities bowels are from Italy and feature an oval design as do the taps. 
The vanity was designed and built by our carpenters and has clever concealed plumbing, enabling full drawers for storgae with no cut outs. 
The porcelain tiles are an oversized 600 by 600. 
A stainless steel drain takes care of the shower and the main floor.
