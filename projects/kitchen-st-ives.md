The new kitchen was totally built on site by our own carpenters, the doors are polyurethane off site but all cabinetry was built on site. 

The kitchen features loads of storage and the latest soft closing doors & drawers. 

Even the glass splash backs & stone tops were installed by our carpenters. 

There is a large walk in pantry with plenty of shelves.
