1970s house renovated totally, New 6 stacker sliding doors, new bathrooms, staircase, glass railings floating floors and new skirtings & architraves. 

The front door and entry area was totally redesigned. 

The house was then panted inside and the outside was painted using Emerclad so that it would be totally water resistant. 

The main living areas were opened up, with walls removed and new LVL beams placed in place. 

The whole place looked like new when finished and took only 6 weeks to complete.
